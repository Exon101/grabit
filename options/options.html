GrabIt

Version 1.0.0

GrabIt is a privacy-respecting Chrome extension that lets you download videos, images, and audio from YouTube, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, Facebook, Twitch, and Bilibili. All processing happens locally in your browser — no third-party servers, no telemetry.
